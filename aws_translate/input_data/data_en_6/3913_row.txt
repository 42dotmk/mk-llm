How to make an Herbed Egg Sandwich:

Ingredients:

1. 2 slices of bread
2. 1-2 large eggs
3. 2 tbsp unsalted butter
4. Fresh herbs (e.g., parsley, chives, or basil)
5. Salt and pepper
6. Cheese (optional)
7. Mayonnaise and mustard (optional)
8. Lettuce and tomato (optional)

Instructions:

1. Prepare the ingredients: Finely chop the fresh herbs and set aside. If you're adding cheese, lettuce, and tomato, slice the cheese, wash the lettuce, and slice the tomato. Set these ingredients aside as well.

2. Crack the eggs: In a small bowl, crack the eggs and beat them with a fork or whisk until the yolks and whites are combined. Add the chopped herbs, salt, and pepper to the beaten eggs and whisk again to incorporate the herbs evenly.

3. Cook the eggs: In a non-stick frying pan or skillet, melt the butter over low to medium heat. Then, pour the egg mixture into the pan. Cook the eggs gently. If you wish, fold the cooked egg mixture in half or make it an omelette-style by adding the cheese on top of the egg mixture before folding. Let the cheese melt while the eggs cook through. Once the eggs are fully cooked, remove the frying pan from the heat.

4. Toast the bread: While the eggs are cooking, toast the bread slices in a toaster or toaster oven until they are golden brown and crispy. Alternatively, you can use the same frying pan used for the eggs, adding more butter to toast the bread slices on each side.

5. Assemble the sandwich: Spread mayonnaise and mustard on one side of each slice of bread if desired. Place the cooked herbed eggs onto one slice of bread, followed by the cheese if you haven't added it to the eggs already. Add lettuce and tomato slices if you choose to use them. Finally, top the sandwich with the second slice of bread.

6. Serve immediately: Cut the sandwich into halves or quarters, according to your preference, and serve immediately. You can enjoy the herbed egg sandwich as a hearty breakfast or a delicious lunch.
