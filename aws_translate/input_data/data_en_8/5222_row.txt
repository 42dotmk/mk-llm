A: Scarlett Johansson has had an extensive and successful acting career, starring in a variety of movies across different genres. Some of the most notable films in which she has appeared include:

1. Lost in Translation (2003) - Directed by Sofia Coppola, this critically acclaimed film features Johansson as Charlotte, a young woman who forms an unlikely bond with an older actor played by Bill Murray while they are both in Tokyo. 

2. Girl with a Pearl Earring (2003) - Johansson plays Griet, a young maid in the house of Dutch painter Johannes Vermeer, who eventually becomes the subject of one of his most famous paintings.

3. Match Point (2005) - In this Woody Allen-directed film, Johansson portrays Nola, an aspiring actress involved in a dangerous love triangle with a tennis instructor and his wealthy brother-in-law.

4. The Prestige (2006) - Johansson stars as Olivia Wenscombe, an assistant to two rival magicians played by Hugh Jackman and Christian Bale in this Christopher Nolan movie.

5. Iron Man 2 (2010) - This marks Johansson's debut in the Marvel Cinematic Universe as Natasha Romanoff, also known as the Black Widow, who becomes an integral part of the Avengers team.

6. The Avengers (2012) - The first ensemble film featuring Marvel superheroes, with Johansson reprising her role as Black Widow.

7. Don Jon (2013) - Johansson plays Barbara Sugarman, a woman with very specific expectations of her romantic partners, in this film directed by Joseph Gordon-Levitt.

8. Under the Skin (2013) - In this thought-provoking sci-fi film, Johansson portrays an alien who takes on the form of a human woman to prey on unsuspecting men.

9. Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014) - Johansson once again portrays Black Widow, who assists Steve Rogers in uncovering a dangerous conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D.

10. Lucy (2014) - Johansson stars in the title role as a woman who gains extraordinary powers after a drug implanted in her body leaks into her system.

11. Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015) - Johansson's Black Widow helps the Avengers team in their battle against the villainous AI Ultron.

12. Hail, Caesar! (2016) - In this Coen brothers' film, Johansson portrays actress DeeAnna Moran in a star-studded ensemble set during the Golden Age of Hollywood.

13. Captain America: Civil War (2016) - Johansson reprises her role as Black Widow in this superhero film that sees the Avengers divided between the conflicting ideologies of Iron Man and Captain America.

14. Ghost in the Shell (2017) - In this live-action adaptation of the popular Japanese manga and anime, Johansson stars as Major Mira Killian/Motoko Kusanagi, a cyber-enhanced human with a mysterious past.

15. Avengers: Infinity War (2018) - Johansson's Black Widow helps the Avengers and their allies defend the universe against the powerful Thanos.

16. Marriage Story (2019) - In this Netflix original film directed by Noah Baumbach, Johansson plays Nicole, an actress going through a painful divorce from her husband, played by Adam Driver.

17. Jojo Rabbit (2019) - In this satirical film set during World War II, Johansson plays Rosie Betzler, the mother of the titular character.

18. Avengers: Endgame (2019) - Johansson's Black Widow plays a critical role in the Avengers' plan to reverse the catastrophic events of "Infinity War."

These are some of the most notable films featuring Scarlett Johansson, though there are many more movies that she has starred in throughout her career.
