Season 14 of Grey's Anatomy, which aired from September 28, 2017, through May 17, 2018, saw several actors returning to reprise their roles on the popular medical drama series.

1. Ellen Pompeo as Dr. Meredith Grey: As the titular character and protagonist of the series, Ellen Pompeo returned for season 14. She has been a series regular since the show's inception in 2005 and continues to play the role of Dr. Meredith Grey.

2. Justin Chambers as Dr. Alex Karev: Justin Chambers also returned for season 14, playing the role of Dr. Alex Karev, an excellent pediatric surgeon with a complicated personal life. He has been a main cast member since the first season as well.

3. Chandra Wilson as Dr. Miranda Bailey: Playing the role of Dr. Miranda Bailey, the Chief of Surgery at Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital, Chandra Wilson returned for the 14th season. She has been portraying Dr. Bailey since the show's beginning.

4. James Pickens Jr. as Dr. Richard Webber: James Pickens Jr. continued his role as Dr. Richard Webber, a seasoned and experienced general surgeon who serves as a mentor to many of the show's characters.

5. Kevin McKidd as Dr. Owen Hunt: Continuing his role as Dr. Owen Hunt, a former US Army trauma surgeon and Head of Trauma at the hospital, Kevin McKidd returned for the 14th season of Grey's Anatomy.

6. Jessica Capshaw as Dr. Arizona Robbins: Jessica Capshaw returned for season 14 as Dr. Arizona Robbins, a skilled pediatric surgeon and former Head of Fetal Surgery. However, it is essential to mention that her character exits the series at the end of this season.

7. Sarah Drew as Dr. April Kepner: Also returning for season 14 was Sarah Drew, who portrayed Dr. April Kepner, a trauma surgeon who faces several challenges in her personal life during this season. Season 14 marked the final season for Dr. April Kepner as well.

8. Jesse Williams as Dr. Jackson Avery: Jesse Williams continued his role as Dr. Jackson Avery, a talented plastic surgeon and the grandson of the legendary Dr. Harper Avery.

9. Camilla Luddington as Dr. Jo Wilson: Camilla Luddington returned to play Dr. Jo Wilson, a resident who specializes in general surgery and faces numerous challenges in her personal life.

10. Caterina Scorsone as Dr. Amelia Shepherd: Caterina Scorsone reprised her role as Dr. Amelia Shepherd, a neurosurgeon and the sister of the late Dr. Derek Shepherd.

11. Kelly McCreary as Dr. Maggie Pierce: Kelly McCreary continued her role as Dr. Maggie Pierce, the Head of Cardiothoracic Surgery and half-sister of Meredith Grey.

12. Giacomo Gianniotti as Dr. Andrew DeLuca: Giacomo Gianniotti returned as Dr. Andrew DeLuca, a surgical resident who gets romantically involved with several of his fellow doctors.

In addition to these main cast members, several recurring and guest stars also appeared throughout season 14, including Abigail Spencer as Dr. Megan Hunt, Owen's long-lost sister, and Matthew Morrison as Dr. Paul Stadler, Jo's abusive ex-husband. This season also featured the return of Kim Raver as Dr. Teddy Altman, a cardiothoracic surgeon who had left the series after season 8, as a guest star before becoming a main cast member again in season 15.
