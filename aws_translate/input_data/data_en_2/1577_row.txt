Anna Kendrick is a talented American actress and singer who has been in numerous films, television shows, and stage performances throughout her career. Here is a detailed list of some of her most notable appearances:

1. High Society (1998): Anna Kendrick made her acting debut at the tender age of 12, starring as Dinah Lord in this Broadway musical comedy. Her outstanding performance earned her a Theatre World Award and a Tony Award nomination.

2. Camp (2003): Kendrick played the character Fritzi Wagner in this independent musical film about a summer camp dedicated to the performing arts. It was this performance that garnered her wider recognition within the industry.

3. Rocket Science (2007): Kendrick appeared as the character Ginny Ryerson, a talented high school student and debate team leader in this independent comedy-drama film. The role earned her a nomination for an Independent Spirit Award.

4. Twilight (2008): This popular romantic fantasy film marked Kendrick's first foray into mainstream cinema. She portrayed Jessica Stanley, a high school student and friend of the film's protagonist, Bella Swan. Kendrick featured in all four subsequent Twilight film series installments as well.

5. Up in the Air (2009): Anna Kendrick starred alongside George Clooney and Vera Farmiga in this critically-acclaimed dramatic comedy. Her role as Natalie Keener, an ambitious and career-driven woman, garnered her widespread praise and multiple award nominations, including Best Supporting Actress nominations at the Academy Awards, Golden Globe Awards, and BAFTA Awards.

6. Scott Pilgrim vs. the World (2010): Kendrick played the character Stacey Pilgrim, the protagonist's sister, in this action-comedy.

7. Pitch Perfect (2012): One of Kendrick's most iconic roles, she starred as Beca Mitchell in this successful musical comedy. Not only did she showcase her impressive vocal skills, but her performance of "Cups" became a chart-topping hit. Kendrick reprised the role in Pitch Perfect 2 (2015) and Pitch Perfect 3 (2017).

8. Into the Woods (2014): In this movie adaptation of the Broadway musical, Kendrick portrayed Cinderella alongside a star-studded cast featuring Meryl Streep, Emily Blunt, Chris Pine, and Johnny Depp.

9. The Last Five Years (2014): Kendrick starred in this musical drama film as Cathy Hiatt, a struggling actress whose marriage is falling apart. She showcased her versatile acting and singing abilities throughout the movie.

10. Trolls (2016): In this animated film, Kendrick lent her voice to the protagonist, Princess Poppy. She returned for the film's sequel, Trolls World Tour (2020).

In addition to these films, Anna Kendrick has appeared in numerous other movies, including 50/50 (2011), End of Watch (2012), Cake (2014), Mike and Dave Need Wedding Dates (2016), Table 19 (2017), and A Simple Favor (2018). She has also made television appearances in series such as Viva Laughlin (2007), Fear Itself (2008), and the leading role in Love Life (2020).

Throughout her career, Anna Kendrick has been recognized for her diverse range of performances and vocal talent, proving that she is a force to be reckoned with in the entertainment industry.
